SMSpotify

A text-message based Spotify DJ system. Designed from the ground up to allow users to easily contribute to Spotify by texting the name of a song or artist to a Twilio phone number. Get ready for a collaborative playlist experience like no other.

The Team

Andrew Braunstein is a Junior in the School of Engineering and Applied Science at the University of Pennsylvania. You can find out more here.

Technologies

SMSpotify is built in Ruby using the Sinatra framework. It utilizes Spotify's metadata API, libspotify, and Hallon for search and Spotify API calls. Also without Twilio's amazing API, SMSpotify would be nothing.
